Maharajas' Express


The Maharaja Express is a luxury tourist train in India, renowned for its opulent travel experience and royal ambiance. Launched in 2010, the train offers a unique blend of modern amenities and a journey reminiscent of the regal era of Indian maharajas. Operated by Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC), the Maharaja Express traverses through some of the most iconic destinations in India, covering popular tourist circuits.

Golden Chariot

The Golden Chariot connects major tourist areas in Karnataka, Kerala, Goa, Tamil Nadu, and Puducherry weekly. Named after the stone chariot at Hampi's Vitthala temple, the train's 19 purple-and-gold coaches feature a logo depicting a mythological animal with the head of an elephant and the body of a lion. Its amenities are inspired by the Palace on Wheels.the Golden Chariot offers hospitality by the Mapple Group.
